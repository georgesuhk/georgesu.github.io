Exploring the universe with computation.

I mainly develop simulations of complex physical systems, especially quantum systems. Simply put, it's fun to play God sometimes. I am drawn to the concepts of simulating randomness, such as stochastic models and monte-carlo methods, and also the possibility of simulating quantum systems using quantum computation. Furthermore, I am interested in applying machine learning to computational science, especially the formulation of probabilistic numerics.
